Common Questions

Which aligner brand is right for me?
That's exactly what your free consultation is for! Dr. Kauffman will evaluate your teeth and recommend the best option—whether that's Invisalign, 3M Clarity, or SureSmile—based on your specific case and budget.
How long does treatment take?
Most patients complete treatment in 12-18 months, though some mild cases can be done in as little as 6 months. You'll get a personalized timeline at your consultation.
What if I have dental insurance?
Great news—we accept most dental PPO plans! We'll check your orthodontic benefits and apply any coverage toward your treatment. Combined with the $500 discount, your out-of-pocket cost may be lower than you think.
Is financing really 0%?
Yes! Through OrthoBank, qualified patients can get 0% interest financing. We also accept CareCredit. We'll go over all your options so you can choose what works best.
